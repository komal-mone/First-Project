Categories Of Our Chaha Products

Green Tea

Green tea is a minimally oxidized tea known for its fresh, grassy flavor and high antioxidant content.

Nilgiri Tea

Nilgiri tea is a fragrant, flavorful black tea grown in the Nilgiri Hills of southern India, known for its briskness and floral aroma.

Black Tea

Black tea is a fully oxidized tea known for its robust flavor, dark color, and higher caffeine content compared to other teas.

Assam Tea

Assam tea is a bold, malty black tea grown in India's Assam region, known for its strong flavor and bright color.
